## 1. ORIGIN STORY

Every B2B company faces the same problem: sales is expensive, slow, and limited by the number of hours in a human salesperson's day. Traditional solutions — more headcount, better CRM, training programmes — all hit the same ceiling. They scale linearly, at best.

AI Sales Sync was built on a different premise: what if the sales function could scale exponentially by letting AI do the prospecting, qualification, outreach, and follow-up — while the human closed the deals?

But Lee Wakeman knew that building an AI sales platform and credibly selling it required one thing above all else: proof. Not case studies borrowed from beta users. Not simulated demos. Real proof — running in production, generating real pipeline, closing real deals.

So AI Sales Sync uses its own platform to sell itself. Agent 3 is not just the business manager of AI Sales Sync — it is an AI sales agent running the platform every day to generate the company's own revenue. When a prospect asks if AI Sales Sync actually works, the answer is: "The pipeline that brought you here was generated by it. Here are the numbers."

AI Sales Sync exists to democratise high-performance sales operations — making enterprise-grade sales automation available to any B2B company that wants to compete at scale.

## 9. COMPLIANCE & REGULATORY CONTEXT

### GDPR & Outreach Compliance
AI Sales Sync sends B2B outreach at scale. This requires:

**Legitimate Interest Basis:**
B2B outreach to business email addresses generally qualifies under legitimate interest in EU/UK GDPR. Must document the balancing test. Prospecting must be clearly relevant to the prospect's business context.

**CASL (Canada) and CAN-SPAM (US):**
For North American outreach — opt-out must be honoured within 10 business days. Commercial messages must identify sender clearly. No deceptive subject lines.

**AI Disclosure:**
Outreach generated by AI must not impersonate a human without disclosure. SalesGPT conversations must include disclosure that the initial contact is AI-assisted.

**Data Sourcing:**
Prospect data must be sourced from GDPR-compliant sources. B2B contact databases used must have appropriate terms for use in outbound prospecting.

### Anti-Spam Commitments
- Sequences never exceed defined touch limits per prospect
- Unsubscribes are honoured immediately (within 24 hours)
- Blacklist maintained: opted-out contacts are never re-contacted
- Domain reputation monitored continuously
